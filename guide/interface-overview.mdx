Gantt Maker features an intuitive dual-mode interface designed for visual project planning. The interface is organized into distinct sections that work together to provide a comprehensive project management experience.

## Main Layout Sections

The interface is divided into four primary areas that enable efficient project visualization and management:

### Timeline View Area

The timeline view is the central component where your project schedule comes to life. This interactive area displays:

- **Visual task bars** color-coded by team member assignment
- **Time scale headers** showing dates or hours depending on your selected mode
- **Task dependencies and overlaps** displayed in real-time
- **Progress indicators** showing task status (Not Started, In Progress, Completed)

<Tip>
Click directly on any task bar to cycle through progress states: Not Started → WIP (In Progress) → Completed
</Tip>

### Team Member Panel

The team panel provides visual management of project participants:

- **Color-coded member cards** with unique colors for easy identification
- **Role and specialty information** displayed for each team member
- **Drag-and-drop reassignment** to quickly change task ownership
- **Quick add/edit controls** for managing team composition

Each team member is automatically assigned a unique color that carries through to their tasks in the timeline view, making it easy to see workload distribution at a glance.

### Task List and Configuration Area

The task configuration section allows you to:

- **Define tasks** with clear descriptions and details
- **Assign team members** to specific tasks
- **Set task duration** and scheduling parameters
- **Configure task properties** and metadata
- **Reorder tasks** to match project priorities

<Note>
All changes are automatically saved to your browser's local storage, ensuring you never lose your work.
</Note>

### Configuration Panel

The intelligent configuration area provides advanced scheduling options:

- **Weekend exclusion** to skip non-working days automatically
- **Custom date exclusions** for holidays and company closures
- **Time block definitions** for non-working hours
- **Working hours configuration** to match your team's schedule

These settings ensure your timeline reflects realistic working conditions and accurate delivery estimates.

## Navigation Between Views

### Dual Mode System

Gantt Maker offers two distinct timeline modes, accessible with a single click:

**Date Mode**
- Traditional planning by days and weeks
- Ideal for longer-term projects spanning weeks or months
- Displays calendar dates along the timeline
- Automatically accounts for weekend and holiday exclusions

**Hours Mode**
- Precision planning down to the hour
- Perfect for intensive short-term projects or sprints
- Shows hourly breakdowns across days
- Essential for detailed resource allocation

<Tip>
Switch between modes instantly to view your project at different time scales without losing any data.
</Tip>

## Key Interactive Elements

### Real-Time Editing

Every element in the interface supports direct manipulation:

- **Click to edit** task names and descriptions in place
- **Drag task bars** to adjust start dates and duration
- **Drag team members** onto tasks for quick reassignment
- **Click progress indicators** to update task status

### Visual Feedback

The interface provides immediate visual feedback for all actions:

- **Color highlighting** shows selected elements
- **Collision detection** identifies over-assigned team members
- **Progress shading** displays completion percentage
- **Hover tooltips** reveal additional task details

### Smart Algorithms

Behind the scenes, intelligent algorithms enhance your planning:

- **Automatic collision detection** alerts you to scheduling conflicts
- **Realistic timeline generation** based on working hours and exclusions
- **Smart spacing** prevents visual overlap of concurrent tasks
- **Adaptive layout** adjusts to different screen sizes

<Note>
The interface detects over-allocation automatically, helping you identify when team members have conflicting assignments.
</Note>

## Data Management

### Auto-Save

All your project data is automatically saved to your browser's local storage as you work. There's no need to manually save changes.

### Export and Backup

- **Export to JSON** for backup or sharing with team members
- **Multiple project versions** can be maintained separately
- **Import capability** to restore or share project files

<Tip>
Regularly export your project files to create backups outside your browser storage.
</Tip>

## Getting Started

To begin using the interface:

1. **Configure your timeline** by selecting date or hours mode
2. **Add team members** with unique colors and roles
3. **Create tasks** with clear descriptions and assignments
4. **Visualize progress** in the interactive timeline view
5. **Adapt on the fly** with real-time editing and updates

The intuitive design ensures minimal learning curve, allowing you to start planning effectively within minutes.
