Track your project's progress in real-time using Gantt Maker's intuitive status workflow and visual progress indicators.

## Task Status Workflow

Every task follows a clear three-stage progression:

```
Pending → WIP (Work in Progress) → Completed
```

### Status Stages Explained

- **Pending**: Task is planned but work has not yet started
- **WIP (Work in Progress)**: Task is actively being worked on
- **Completed**: Task is finished and delivered

## Updating Task Status

Change task status with a single click:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Locate the Task">
    Find the task you want to update on your Gantt chart.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Click to Update">
    Click directly on the task to cycle through status stages:
    - First click: Changes Pending → WIP
    - Second click: Changes WIP → Completed
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Verify Status Change">
    The task's visual indicator updates immediately to reflect its new status.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Visual Progress Indicators

Gantt Maker provides multiple visual cues for tracking progress:

### Task-Level Indicators

- **Status badges**: Each task displays its current status
- **Color variations**: Different visual treatments for Pending, WIP, and Completed tasks
- **Progress markers**: Clear visual differentiation between status stages

### Timeline Visualization

See progress across your entire project:

- **Completed tasks**: Visually distinguished to show finished work
- **Active work**: WIP tasks highlighted to show current focus areas
- **Upcoming work**: Pending tasks displayed for future planning

## Viewing Overall Project Progress

Get a high-level view of your project's health:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Scan the Chart">
    Look across your Gantt chart to see the distribution of task statuses.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Assess Completion Rate">
    Quickly gauge what percentage of tasks are completed vs. in progress vs. pending based on visual indicators.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Review Timeline">
    Check if completed tasks align with planned schedules and if WIP tasks are on track.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Identifying Bottlenecks

Use progress tracking to spot potential issues:

### Signs of Bottlenecks

- **Clustering of WIP tasks**: Multiple tasks stuck in Work in Progress status may indicate obstacles
- **Delayed completions**: Tasks remaining in WIP beyond their planned duration
- **Pending backlogs**: Large numbers of pending tasks while team capacity is available
- **Uneven distribution**: Some team members with many completed tasks while others show little progress

### Addressing Bottlenecks

<Steps>
  <Step title="Identify the Issue">
    Look for patterns in the visual indicators that suggest blocked or delayed work.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Analyze Causes">
    Determine if bottlenecks are due to resource constraints, dependencies, or other factors.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Reassign or Adjust">
    Use the drag-and-drop reassignment feature to rebalance workload or adjust task durations and timelines.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Monitor Changes">
    Track whether your adjustments resolve the bottleneck by watching status updates.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Real-Time Progress Updates

All progress changes are reflected immediately:

- **Instant visualization**: Status updates appear in real-time across the interface
- **Auto-save**: Progress changes are automatically saved to local storage
- **No delay**: Click and see results immediately without page refreshes

<Note>
Progress data is stored locally in your browser, ensuring your project information remains private and accessible offline.
</Note>

## Best Practices

- **Regular updates**: Encourage team members to update task status frequently
- **Honest reporting**: Ensure status reflects actual progress, not wishful thinking
- **Daily reviews**: Check progress indicators daily to catch issues early
- **Team visibility**: Use the visual progress to facilitate stand-ups and team discussions
- **Pattern recognition**: Learn to spot your team's typical bottleneck patterns and address them proactively
- **Celebrate milestones**: Use completed task visualization to recognize team achievements
